Executive Deluxe

Elegant, luxurious, and comfortable – the Executive Deluxe provides the perfect setting to relax after a long day of work. The suite features a luxurious queen-sized bed with a spacious closet, combination of wood and marble flooring as well private balconies in some suites. There are floor-to-ceiling windows to brighten up the room in natural light and create a warm ambience, while the double-glass panes block the heat and noise out. For all your business needs, all rooms come with a work desk and Wi-Fi with additional LAN connection as standard. The spacious bathroom features shower enclosure, as well as a separate bathtub and comes with all the necessary features as standard. For your entertainment, there is a 46” LED television with wide selection of satellite channels that you can access from the comfort of your bed or even while in the bath.
